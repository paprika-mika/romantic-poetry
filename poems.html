LONGING

Today we are singing
Tomorrow there'll be tears
My soul, are you walking
Away from me again, longing?
Everything was forgotten
Silence filled my heart
How could I ever measure
Whenever you'll finally take a rest
Oh longing, when will your stirring in my heart stop
So that it may look through the eyes of dreams
Into heaven



STORY OF A SAD AUTUMN NIGHT

From every branch it is dripping
And misty veils are bowing gently
Reddish flowers are turning white
Shiver and die in the ice
The morning sun is shining on
Bleached pleasures crying



IT WAS A LOVELY SUMMERS DAY

It was a lovely summers day
The sun was shining
While I was laying at your feet
Just with our love
We didn't see the flowers blooming
We only looked in our eyes so deep
The sun was laughing happy
And in the heart slept all the pain
But for you it was nothing but a game
You didn't want to understand my plead
And when the first autumn leaves did fall
I left you and started walking again



THE FRIEND

Let us once again walk the quiet roads
Those we went often together in former times
Even though the birds are not singing any more
And the autumn winds will blow wildly through the trees
Both if us feeling the same pain and bliss
Timidly holding our hands
No, the time didn't take you away from mee
But not one day, that ever was, will return
Aren't we both like mountain sea and woods?
I have born myself into you
You did loose yourself in my landscape
And we are growing - but never grow old



DECISION

Goodbye woman - goodbye - child
The bliss is not on our side
Something higher is calling out for me, something you can't see
But so strong it will tear us apart
Again I want to be the dreamer
The one they don't understand
With weak a body but strong a soul
Shaking the pillars of the injustice
I want to stand by my brothers and speak free
To kings and peasants
Speak the truth facing procecution in court
Or behind dungeon walls
And when there will be a dangerous threat for all people
No woman with kids
Shall wail in fear when I want to act
Trying to hold and stop me
Goodbye woman- goodbye- child!!
Duty is calling, waiting for me on earth
The muze is calling as is the world
I have to become a brother, a Guttzeit again



MAX KLINGER

Yesterday I walked through flower meadows
And I was dreaming instantly of paradises
With naked beauties inside
Sliding dazzled through sunny fields
And there was this sound
Swimming around everything
Like the songs of the sirenes
And it was not really a landscape but rather a fragrance
Smelling of the earthiest earth
And I thought now you almost reached
Divine humanity
